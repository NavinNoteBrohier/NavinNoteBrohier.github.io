Bear Handed Fishing

An Unreal Engine 4 VR project made during an Academy of Interactive Entertainment Game Jam over three days.

Made in a team of seven, two Programmers, two Game Designers and 3 Artists. The theme of the jam was 'Elasticity' and we decided to experiment in VR

The game has you playing as a bear with extendable elastic arms, you have 2 minutes to punch as many fish as possible

I worked on the Player Controller and the VR Implementation

We exhibited this game at the AIE Booth at Oz Comic Con alongside other Year 2 Major works and at an AIE Open day

Bolter Shooting Range

A Work in progress started in Unreal Engine 4.18

Made during an evening to demonstrate and experiment with an object class for VR interactable Object.

The Bolters and the magazines all use the same object class just with different presets and extra functions

The art was mostly made with primitive objects and Unreal brush geometry that can be converted to static meshes through the engine.

The ork sprite was sourced off Google Images
